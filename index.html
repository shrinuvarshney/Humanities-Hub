Why Humanities is a Superpower

It's more than just subjects—it's about building essential skills for any career in the 21st century.

Think Like a Pro

Learn to analyze complex problems, see patterns others miss, and build strong, persuasive arguments. This is a skill every top company looks for.

Communicate with Impact

Master the art of storytelling. Learn to write and speak in a way that captures attention, changes minds, and gets your ideas across clearly.

Understand People

Develop empathy and a deep understanding of human behavior, cultures, and societies. This is key to success in leadership, marketing, and more.

Career Spotlight: Beyond the Obvious

Think humanities is just about teaching or research? Think again. Here are some cool, modern jobs where humanities grads excel.

UX Writer

Craft the words you see in apps and websites. You'll use your understanding of language and psychology to make technology easier and more enjoyable for everyone.

Skills: Empathy, Clear Writing

Policy Analyst

Work for the government or NGOs to research and improve our society. You'll use your critical thinking skills to help solve real-world problems.

Skills: Research, Analysis

Digital Marketer

Use your understanding of culture and communication to create compelling online campaigns for brands. You'll tell stories that connect with people.

Skills: Creativity, Communication

Cracking the Admissions Process

The CUET exam is your key to top universities. Here's what you need to know.

CUET Score Goals (Based on 2025 Cutoffs)

With the release of the 2025 cutoffs, it's clear the competition for top colleges is intense. For premier DU colleges, scores for popular humanities courses are now calculated out of 1000. To be competitive, you should be aiming for **900+**.

Real 2025 First Round Cutoff Scores:

  • B.A. (Hons) Political Science at Hindu College: 950.58
  • B.A. (Hons) English at St. Stephen's College: 926.92
  • B.A. (Hons) Psychology at LSR: 926.53

Key Takeaway:

Your Class 12th subjects and your CUET score are both crucial. Prepare well for both, and keep an eye on the official DU admission portal (admission.uod.ac.in) for all announcements.
